# VISUAL GUIDELINES for GVL Design Co.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Montserrat
- **Secondary Font**: Open Sans
- **Font Weights**: Regular 400, Bold 700
- **Line Height**: 1.5
- **Font Sizes**:
  - **Headings**: h1 - 36px, h2 - 30px, h3 - 24px
  - **Body**: 16px

## Spacing
- **Margin**: Use 20px margin around all elements for consistent spacing.
- **Padding**: Use 15px padding for buttons and inputs.
- **Grid System**: 12-column grid with 20px gutters.

## Imagery Style
- **Photography**: Use high-resolution images that reflect the brand’s aesthetic.
- **Image Treatment**: Apply a subtle gold overlay to images to maintain brand consistency.
- **Iconography**: Use simple, line-based icons with gold accents.

## Logo Usage
- **Clear Space**: Ensure at least 20px of clear space around the logo.
- **Color Variations**: Use gold on dark backgrounds and white on light backgrounds.
- **Do’s and Don’ts**:
  - Do maintain proportions.
  - Don't alter colors or orientation.
- **3D Aesthetic**: The logo features a metallic gold finish that creates depth. This effect should be reflected in all branding materials where the logo appears. Use shadow effects and gradients to mimic this look.

## Additional Notes
- Ensure consistency in applying typography, spacing, and imagery across all assets.
- Review brand guidelines regularly to align with evolution in design.
